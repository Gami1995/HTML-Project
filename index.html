How To Play DnD In 5 Easy Steps

What is DnD
DnD is a tabletop rpg game that can be played with as many people as you want, you can even play it on your own. The Basics of it is that you make a character that interatcs with a world made by the creators or by your own friends, you can even make your own worlds for you and your friends to explore.
The Basics
DnD is very simple and eay to play, all you need are dice. you can buy a full set of dice for DnD for less than $5, there are 7 different dice needed to play, all with different uses. The most important thing to remember about DnD is that you control the character and define who they are, you can be a hero or villain, It's your choice.
